Welcome to 2021!

Updated: Feburary 17th, 2021

It's been a long year, hasn't it?

We've been working hard to figure out the answer to a pretty basic question: "What do we do about our conference?"

We've been watching our communities – the places where we work, the groups we're a part of, and the larger Agile communities as well – all struggle with, work through, and sort out the ways to collaborate, communicate, and come together during the past year. Through it all, we've been missing the connections we have with you, the greater Richmond-area community of Agile practitioners.

We've been trying to figure out how to maintain the quality and scale of InnoVAte Virginia, the premier regional Agile conference in the greater Central Virginia area, while also staying flexible in the ways we connect during the ongoing pandemic. We've talked about and explored and hoped for a number of different solutions, and like a team of recovering project managers, we've forgotten some of the basics of Being Agile:

  • Release Early, Release Often
  • Experiment and Learn Rapidly
  • "Perfect is the Enemy of the Good"
  • Deliver, Deliver, Deliver

So, it's time to shift and BE AGILE.

We've been working behind the scenes to get back to serving our community; I'm hoping you've been following our progress on updating our website, as well as our shift to using Meetup to manage our monthly meetings. Our latest effort is this: MINnoVAte Virginia Spring 2021.

As the Board has been working to balance our mission and our ability, we realize that the best thing we can do is deliver a small increment of value – "Plan, Do, Check, Act". MINnoVAte Virginia is a one-day, two-track online conference, building on the lessons learned from attending similar conferences and based on what we can confidently deliver with the technology, team, and time we all have.

It all starts, however, with you.

We can't have a conference without great speakers. We've been incredibly fortunate to have a community of smart, compelling speakers for a number of years, and today we're opening our Call for Speakers for MINnoVAte Virginia: Spring 2021.


Braz Brandt
President, Agile Richmond

F.A.Q

  • Is InnoVAte Virginia 2020 Cancelled?

    No! Out of an abundance of caution for our community, and working with local businesses, we have decided to postpone InnoVAte Virginia 2020. We are actively working with our friends at Virginia Crossings to help us choose the best date to reschedule the conference, with an eye toward either the summer or fall of 2020.

  • Do I need to buy a new ticket?

    There's no need to do anything with your ticket right now – all current tickets will be automatically moved to the new date in the coming days.

  • Can I get a refund?

    Of course! When we announce the new date for InnoVAte Virginia 2020, we're hoping you'll still be as excited as we are to attend. However, if there's any reason you can't attend, or if you just want a refund, we understand. You can request a refund through Eventbrite, and contact us if you have any other questions.

  • I'm a speaker. Will I still be able to speak?

    Absolutely! We're excited to have you speak to the greater Richmond community later this year, and your selection as a speaker is still very important and relevant. If you can't speak on the new date, though, please let us know so we can find an alternate speaker.

  • Will you be accepting new speaker proposals?

    No, not at this time. We're hoping all of our speakers will be able to attend and speak at InnoVAte Virginia 2020; if we have any speakers who can't we have a list of great submissions that just missed the cut for this year's conference. If you want to speak to our community, there will be fantastic opportunities throughout the year; please reach out to us so we can add you to our list of interested speakers.

  • I have a question that wasn't answered here; who do I contact?

    While we tried to anticipate all the questions people might have right now, I'm sure we missed some. Feel free to reach out to us at the link below, and we'll get back to you as soon as we can. We're busy working on rescheduling InnoVAte Virginia 2020, so we may be a little slower to respond than we'd like – but we will reply to everyone.
