miniVAte: InnoVAte Virginia Online

Building on what's made InnoVAte Virginia the best Agile conference in the region, and learning from those around us as we all navigate through the world of working from home and video conferences all-day, miniVAte Virginia's Spring 2021 conference is an all-virtual one-day event for everyone in the greater Richmond community.

Recognizing that the past year has been challenging for everyone, in many different ways, miniVAte is a free conference for all attendees – we only ask that you register, so that we can make sure we've allocated enough virtual seats at our video conferencing table!

Anywhere

miniVAte is our first virtual conference, taking place wherever you happen to have access to the Internet.

We'll have three Zoom "channels" of content during the day, making it easy to pick the sessions you want to see and switch between channels in an easy, consistent way.

See the schedule above for channel and time details throughout the day on the 21st!

Anytime

Want to be part of the conversation? miniVAte Virginia will be LIVE! on Friday, May 21st.

Can't make it on Friday? That's okay, too – miniVAte sessions will be recorded and available on Agile Richmond's YouTube Channel shortly after. Make sure you REGISTER NOW so that you stay in-the-know!

CHANNELS?!

We're going to have three concurrent "channels" on Zoom, so you can pick the session that most interests you.

Each channel will follow the format of channelX.innovatevirginia.com, where X is the channel number you want to join.

The morning's Keynote will be on keynote.innovatevirginia.com, and we'll have a support channel running on minivate-support.innovatevirginia.com.

Channels will be live on the morning of Friday, May 21st.

MOBILE, TOO

Yup. We've got a mobile application, too.

You can view our mobile application at mobile.innovatevirginia.com – it's a Progressive Web App, so you can install it on your device's home screen. Desktop or mobile, we've got you covered!
